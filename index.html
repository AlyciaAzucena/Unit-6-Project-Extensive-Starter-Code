Welcome to Elfhame! You wake up in a magical forest by a faerie. He tells you that you are in the land of Elfhame. You can follow him for help or go down the path for home by yourself.

The creature called “the Roach” guided you to the palace. There Queen Jude gives you magical seeds. Click over her to plant them.

You walk on a random path and find yourself at a nice estate. There you find a women name Taryn and you can double click the estate to follow her.

YAY! Your magical seeds turned into steeds that wil fly you home! Good Job :)

Sadly, Taryn captured you and you are now stuck as her servant forever. ;(
